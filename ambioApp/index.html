Ambio - Capstone Spring 2017

Ambio is a modern take on the classic mood ring. Using the biometric tracking capabilities of wearable technology, Ambio interprets your emotions and shares them with the people closest to you.

My Role

The Ambio team consists of five designers and three developers. I'm leading the charge as manager of the development team.
