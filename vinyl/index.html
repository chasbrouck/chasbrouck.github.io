The Watchrock - Vinyl

In October of 2016, I released a small folk EP under the name "Conner & The Watchrock". To accompany the EP's release, I built an interactive website which simulates the experience of a vinyl record player.

The interactive record player was built entirely with web canvas and heavily relies on jquery. I also utilized the howler.js library for advanced audio customization.

Take A Look: http://chasbrouck.com/thewatchrock/
